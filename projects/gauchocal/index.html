Gauchocal by Dana Nguyen and Gareth George

A calendar for gauchos!

Easily synchronize courses you want from UCSB GOLD with Google Calendar and finds schedule conflicts. May the course be with you. Gauchocal is an extension to UCSB’s GOLD course catalog allowing for quick and easy synchronization of your schedule with Google Calendar. It provides easy tools for copying your schedule onto google calendar as well as viewing the courses that conflict with what you are already planning to take.
